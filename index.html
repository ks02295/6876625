Introduction 

In the last 100 years, we have explored the many wonders of our universe – from the precise orbit of the planets in our 
solar system to the furthest galaxy away from us. However, all the matter we can see and have researched on only makes up 
5% of how much we truly have explored our universe. One of the biggest mysteries that scientists are struggling to solve 
is the existence of dark matter and dark energy where dark matter involves gravity to form galaxies/galaxy clusters by 
sticking together gas, dust and stars together while dark energy is involved in accelerating the expansion of the universe.   
This will be further discussed throughout where we see how these two ideas have come to be, how we can detect dark matter and 
energy and if there’s any technology we can use to strengthen the evidence around these two mysterious substances.
